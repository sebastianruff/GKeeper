Notes from Google Keep

Connect your Google Keep account

Please enter your Google email and a Google Master Token. These credentials are stored on the server for this app instance.

  1. Open Google Account Security.
  2. Enable 2-Step Verification if it is not enabled yet.
  3. Open App passwords.
  4. Create a new app password (or master token) for this app and copy it.
  5. Paste the token below and save the configuration.
